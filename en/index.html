Frequently Asked Questions

What services do you offer?

We offer professional translation and interpretation services between Slovak and Russian, including document translation, certified translations, and live interpretation for meetings, conferences, and other events.

How do I request a translation?

You can request a translation by filling out the online form on our website, uploading the document you need translated, and specifying the language pair (Slovak to Russian or Russian to Slovak

What types of documents can you translate?

We can translate a wide range of documents, including legal documents, medical records, business contracts, technical manuals, academic papers, and personal correspondence.

How long does it take to translate a document?

The turnaround time depends on the length and complexity of the document. Typically, small documents (up to 2,000 words) can be translated within 1-2 business days.

Do you provide certified translations?

Yes, we offer certified translations that are accepted by governmental and legal authorities. Please specify if you need a certified translation when you place your order.

What are your rates for translation services?

Our rates vary based on the type of document, the level of complexity, and the urgency of the request. Please contact us for a personalized quote or check our price-list 

Can I get a quote before placing an order?

Yes, we provide free, no-obligation quotes. Simply upload your document on our website and provide the necessary details to receive an estimate.

How do you ensure the quality of your translations?

Our translations are performed by native speakers and professional translators with expertise in various fields. Each translation is also reviewed by a second linguist to ensure accuracy and quality.

What interpretation services do you offer?

We offer both consecutive and simultaneous interpretation services for meetings, conferences, seminars, and other events. We can provide interpreters in-person or remotely via phone or video call.

How do I book an interpreter?

You can book an interpreter by contacting us through our website or by phone. Please provide details about the event, including the date, location, duration, and any specific requirements.

What are your rates for interpretation services?

Interpretation rates depend on the type of service (consecutive or simultaneous), the duration of the event, and any special requirements. Contact us for a detailed quote

Do you offer translation and interpretation for specific industries?

Yes, we have experts in various industries, including legal, medical, technical, financial, and academic fields. Our translators and interpreters are familiar with industry-specific terminology.

Can you handle urgent translation requests?

Yes, we offer expedited translation services for urgent requests. Additional fees may apply for rush orders. Please contact us as soon as possible to discuss your needs.

How do you handle confidentiality and data security?

We take confidentiality and data security very seriously. All documents are handled with strict confidentiality, and our secure system ensures that your information is protected at all times.

What if I'm not satisfied with the translation or interpretation service?

Customer satisfaction is our top priority. If you are not satisfied with our service, please contact us within 7 days, and we will work with you to address any concerns or make necessary revisions.
